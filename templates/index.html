How AlzDetect Works

AlzDetect leverages a robust AI pipeline to analyze brain MRI scans for Alzheimer's disease classification. Here's how the process works:

1. Preprocessing

Uploaded brain scans undergo skull stripping to remove non-brain tissues, followed by median filtering to reduce noise and enhance scan quality.

2. Data Balancing

We use SMOTETomek, a hybrid resampling method, to balance the dataset by oversampling minority classes and cleaning noisy samples.

3. Feature Extraction

DenseNet121, a convolutional neural network, extracts deep features from the preprocessed scans, capturing complex patterns indicative of Alzheimer’s stages.

4. Classification

An SVM (Support Vector Machine) classifier analyzes the extracted features to predict the category of Alzheimer’s disease.
